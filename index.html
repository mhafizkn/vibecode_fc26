About CORES

Corporate & Engineering Services (CORES) keeps the business running — facilities, workplace experience, corporate security, health & safety, and vendor management all sit under one roof. We build the environment everyone else works in.

This championship started as a lunchtime debate about squad-building and turned into our department's first proper tournament. It's not about the match — it's a platform to bring together professionals from different areas of the department; bringing closely together, strengthening the bonds.

Every match is played on a single shared PS5 station, one controller, no excuses. Results feed straight into the live group tables on the previous page.
